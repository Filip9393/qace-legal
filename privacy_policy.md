## 1. Introduction

Welcome to QAce: Ace your QA interview ("App", "we", "our", "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application available on iOS and Android.

We are committed to protecting your privacy. QAce is designed to work without collecting any personal data.

---

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Information stored locally on your device

QAce stores the following data **only on your device** using local storage (AsyncStorage). This data never leaves your device and is never transmitted to our servers or any third party:

- **Language preference** — your chosen interface language (English or Ukrainian)
- **Theme preference** — dark or light mode setting
- **Quiz statistics** — number of quizzes completed and your last quiz result

### 2.2 Information we do NOT collect

We do not collect, store, or process:

- Personal identification information (name, email, phone number)
- Location data
- Device identifiers or hardware information
- Payment or financial information
- Usage analytics or behavioral data
- Crash reports or diagnostic data
- Any data that could identify you personally

---

## 3. How We Use Information

The locally stored data is used solely to:

- Remember your language and theme preferences between app sessions
- Display your quiz progress on the home screen

This data is never used for advertising, analytics, or any other purpose.

---

## 4. Data Sharing

We do not share any data with third parties because we do not collect any data that could be shared.

QAce does not use:
- Analytics services (Google Analytics, Firebase, etc.)
- Advertising networks
- Social media integrations
- Third-party tracking tools

---

## 5. Data Storage and Security

All data is stored locally on your device. We have no access to this data. If you uninstall the app, all locally stored data is permanently deleted.

---

## 6. Children's Privacy

QAce is not directed at children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect any information from children. The app contains educational content suitable for adults preparing for professional QA engineering interviews.

---

## 7. Future Updates

In future versions of QAce, we may introduce features that require additional data collection, such as:

- User accounts and cloud sync
- In-app purchases
- Push notifications
- Analytics to improve app performance

If we introduce such features, this Privacy Policy will be updated before the release, and you will be notified through the app.

---

## 8. Your Rights

Since we do not collect any personal data, there is no personal information for us to provide, correct, or delete. If you wish to remove all locally stored preferences, simply uninstall the app.

---

## 9. Third-Party Services

The current version of QAce does not integrate any third-party services.
