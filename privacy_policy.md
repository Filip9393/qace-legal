# Privacy Policy — QAce: Ace Your QA Interview

Last updated: April 13, 2026

📋 **Short version:** QAce stores preferences locally on your device. The free version shows ads via Google AdMob. Pro version purchases are processed via Apple and managed by RevenueCat.

## 1. Introduction

Welcome to **QAce: Ace Your QA Interview** ("App", "we", "our"). This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application available on iOS and Android.

We are committed to protecting your privacy.

---

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Data stored locally on your device

QAce stores the following data **only on your device** using local storage (AsyncStorage). This data never leaves your device and is never transmitted to our servers:

* **Language preference** — your chosen interface language (English or Ukrainian)
* **Theme preference** — dark or light mode setting
* **Quiz statistics** — number of quizzes completed and your last quiz result

### 2.2 In-App Purchases (QAce Pro)

If you purchase QAce Pro, the transaction is processed by **Apple (App Store)**. We use **RevenueCat** to verify and manage your purchase entitlement. RevenueCat may collect:

* Purchase history
* Device identifiers
* App usage data related to purchases

RevenueCat does not collect personal identification information. Their privacy policy is available at: https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy

### 2.3 Advertising (Free Version)

The free version of QAce displays advertisements provided by **Google AdMob**. AdMob may collect:

* Advertising identifiers (IDFA on iOS)
* Device information
* Approximate location (based on IP address)
* App usage data for ad targeting

You can opt out of personalized advertising at any time:

**iOS:** Settings → Privacy & Security → Apple Advertising → Turn off Personalized Ads

Google's privacy policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

### 2.4 Information we do NOT collect

We do not collect:

* Personal identification information (name, email, phone number)
* Precise location data
* Payment or financial information
* Crash reports or diagnostic data
* Any data that could identify you personally

---

## 3. How We Use Information

The locally stored data is used solely to:

* Remember your language and theme preferences between app sessions
* Display your quiz progress on the home screen

Purchase data is used to unlock the Pro version and remove ads.

---

## 4. Data Sharing

We share data only with the following third-party services:

* **Apple** — for processing App Store purchases
* **RevenueCat** — for purchase verification and entitlement management
* **Google AdMob** — for displaying advertisements in the free version

We do not sell your personal data to any third parties.

---

## 5. Data Storage and Security

All quiz preferences and statistics are stored locally on your device. We have no access to this data. If you uninstall the app, all locally stored data is permanently deleted.

---

## 6. Children's Privacy

QAce is not directed at children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect any information from children. The app contains educational content suitable for adults preparing for professional QA engineering interviews.

---

## 7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

* Opt out of personalized advertising via your device settings
* Request deletion of data held by our third-party partners (RevenueCat, Google)
* Remove all locally stored preferences by uninstalling the app
* Contact us with any privacy concerns
